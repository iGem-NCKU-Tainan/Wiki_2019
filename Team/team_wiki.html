NCKU_Tainan iGEM Team is established in 2015. In these years, former team members all worked hard in the iGEM competition. 2019 NCKU_Tainan iGEM Team gather in 2019 January. We come from different departments of college, but willing to understanding how to apply synthetic biology into solving real world problems. We prepared and did our best this year, and hope our project can make the world better.

Principle Instructor

I-Son Ng

Associate Professor - Department of Chemical Engineering
She is devoted to the study of synthetic biology, genetic and enzymatic engineering, biorefinery and bioremediation. She has developed a robust and efficient platform to explore the novel and functional enzymes by genetic and proteomics approach.

I-Hsiu Huang

Assistant Professor - Department of Microbiology and Immunology
His lab aims to identify novel therapeutic and preventive measures against Clostridium difficile, a leading cause of antibiotic-associated diarrhea worldwide. In addition, he is also working on identifying the role of oral microbiome in oral cancer progression and proliferation.
