Extraction of total RNA


1. Pulverize hyphae in liquid nitrogen, collect two spoons of powder into 1.5ml EPmicrocentrifuge tube.

2. Add 1ml RNAiso Plus(TAKARA), then homogenate

3. Sit at room temperature for 5 minutes

4. Centrifuge at 4℃ and 12,000g for 5 minutes

5. Add 200µL chloroform, mix thoroughly

6. Centrifuge at 4℃ and 12,000g for 15 minutes

7. Transfer supernatant into new 1.5 mL microcentrifuge tube

8. Add 1ml isopropanol, sit at room temperature for 10 minutes

9. Centrifuge at 4℃ and 12,000g for 10 minutes

10. Add 1mL 75% ethanol to wash the precipitate

11. Centrifuge at 4℃ and 7,500g for 5 minutes

12. Discard the supernatant

13. Open the cap, let sit at room temperature until ethanol volatile completely

14. Dissolve precipitate with moderate RNAase-free H₂O
